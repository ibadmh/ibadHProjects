Tree Maintenance Services

Services:

The maintenance of your tree is essential to the aesthetics and health of the garden or lawn on your property. We offer many Tree related services from planting, pruning, fertilization, and more!

  • Pruning

    Regular pruning is required to keep a tree's growth, structure, and integrity. A properly trimmed tree reduces the likelihood of broken limbs and falling branches, as well as the spread of diseases. Our Arborists provide expert tree pruning and trimming services across Alberta. We ensure that trees and bushes remain healthy for many years to come because we are aware of the effects that various treatments have on them. We also pay close attention to the season, weather, and other factors that may influence the growth of your tree.

  • Planting

    When looking for tree planting services, the best place to start is with an arborist who is knowledgeable about the local tree species. We regularly remind our clients that proper planting is critical to a tree's safe and healthy growth. Before planting, our arborists will purchase the trees from local nurseries to ensure they have been exposed to a similar climate. We enjoy serving our residential and commercial clients with their tree planting needs.

  • Stump Grinding

    Is the process of turning huge surface roots and tree stumps around them into woodchips at a depth of 18 to 22 inches below grade. We typically advise tree and stump removal to assist prevent suckering while suckering trees are in full leaf.

  • Deep Root Fertilization

    Deep root fertilization, in essence, facilitates nutrient transport to the root system, where it is most needed. We recommend applying our all-purpose tree fertilizer in both the spring and fall. Its composition is made up of 10% nitrogen, 10% phosphate, and 10% other active nutrients.

  • Tree Surgery

    By installing threaded steel rods and/or high-strength cables in your tree, you can both repair damage that has already been done and avoid future harm by maintaining the tree's structural integrity.

  • Pesticide and Insect Treatment

    Our experts will analyze your tree, and, if possible, will help remove all harmfull pests and insects that mightve inhabited it throughout the years

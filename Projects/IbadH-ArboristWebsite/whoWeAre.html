Who We Are

One of Alberta's major arborist groups has developed out of what started as a tiny family business. Our modest beginnings and straightforward purpose were to enhance the look and health of YOUR lawn. If you require tree care services in Alberta, go to The Tree Keepers. Since 1995, we have devoted our lives to helping our residential, business, and municipal clients maintain lovely, lush green landscapes. To schedule a meeting, contact us immediately. We anticipate hearing from you. You can get help from our arborists at any of our three home branches. We take pride in providing premium services at competitive rates. With more than 100 full-time staff and more than 25 years in the business, our company is able to completely satisfy every client. We collaborate closely with our clients to finish the work correctly from the initial consultation through to completion.
